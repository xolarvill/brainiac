# Comparison

## Variant Comparison

- M: best for small dogs from 5 to 12 kg.
- L: best for medium dogs from 10 to 25 kg.
- XL: best for larger dogs from 20 to 40 kg.

## Compared With Basic Pillow Beds

This product emphasizes supportive foam, pressure-point comfort, and a removable washable cover. Avoid claiming medical treatment benefits.

## Compared With Outdoor Beds

This product is for indoor resting and should not be used outdoors in rain.
