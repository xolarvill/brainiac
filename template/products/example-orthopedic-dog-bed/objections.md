# Objections

## It costs more than a basic bed.

Position it as a supportive indoor resting product with high-density foam, a removable washable cover, and size guidance for different dogs.

## My dog destroys beds.

Do not recommend this product for aggressive chewing dogs. Suggest choosing a chew-resistant product if chewing is the main concern.

## I am unsure about size.

Ask for the dog's weight, breed, and sleeping posture. Use SKU ranges in `variants.yaml`, then recommend sizing up if the dog sprawls.
