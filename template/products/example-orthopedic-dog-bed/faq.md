# FAQ

## Can this bed help my senior dog feel more comfortable?

Yes. The bed is designed to support joint comfort and help reduce pressure points during indoor rest. For diagnosed medical conditions, customers should consult a veterinarian.

## Can this bed cure arthritis?

No. This bed cannot cure arthritis or any medical condition. It may provide supportive comfort, but medical questions should be handled by a veterinarian.

## Can I wash the foam?

No. Only the removable polyester cover is machine washable. The foam core should not be machine washed.

## Is it suitable for dogs that chew aggressively?

No. It is not suitable for aggressive chewing dogs.

## Which size should I choose?

Use the weight range and dimensions in `variants.yaml`. If the dog is between sizes or likes to stretch out, suggest the larger size.
