# Compliance

## Allowed Claims

- Supports joint comfort.
- Helps reduce pressure points.
- Designed for senior dogs.
- Removable washable cover.

## Evidence-Required Claims

- Veterinarian recommended.
- Clinically tested.
- Orthopedic-grade foam.

Do not use evidence-required claims unless a source file explicitly supports them.

## Forbidden Claims

- cures arthritis
- treats hip dysplasia
- guaranteed pain relief

## Boundary

Customer support and marketing agents must not provide veterinary advice. For medical conditions, recommend consulting a veterinarian.
