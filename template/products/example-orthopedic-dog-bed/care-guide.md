# Care Guide

## Setup

Unpack the compressed bed and allow the foam to expand before use.

## Cleaning

Remove the polyester cover and machine wash it on a gentle cycle. Do not machine wash the foam core.

## Safety

Use indoors on a dry surface. Keep away from rain and standing water. Inspect the cover if the dog scratches or chews heavily.

## Maintenance

Reinstall the cover only after it is fully dry. Rotate the bed occasionally if one side receives heavier use.
